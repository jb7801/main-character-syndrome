What are the Side Affects of Main Character Syndrome?

If you or a love one is suffering from main character syndrom, here are some side affects to look out for. They may not show up all at once when affected, and can gradually develop over time. The best way to prevent them from worsening is to get ahead of the problem.

Side Affects May Include...

Sense of Self-Importance

The dangers of having an exaggerated sense of self-importance, coupled with an overreliance on social media engagement and a dissonance between online and real-life personas, are multifaceted and deeply concerning. When individuals prioritize their online presence over real-world interactions, they risk losing touch with the tangible, immediate world around them. This often leads to a lack of spatial awareness and an inadvertent inconvenience to others, as the virtual world takes precedence over the physical one.
